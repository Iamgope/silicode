About Silicode

We are a passionate group of college friends who have ventured into different areas of the tech industry, working at leading companies worldwide. Our diverse experiences span software development, hardware engineering, AI research, and product innovation.

United by our shared love for technology, we believe in the power of community-driven learning and collaborative growth. Whether you're just starting your tech journey or you're a seasoned professional, there's a place for you in our community.

Our mission is to create a space where curious minds can explore, learn, and build the next generation of technological solutions together.
